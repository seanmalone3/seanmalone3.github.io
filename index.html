About Me


I am a Full-Stack Data Scientist with over 8 years of experience in acquiring data, building pipelines, maintaining data warehouses, producing value through analytics, deploying models, and leading data teams. I most recently built the Data Science department at Jiffyshirts.com, an mid-sized e-commerce company in the soft goods space for small business screen printers in North America.

Some of my favorite projets have been building out a competitive pricing system for over 300k SKUs, using gradient boosted decision trees to predict customer churn/lifetime value, and creating a predictive inventory score to show stock-out risk to customers during the pandemic supply chain crisis.
